1. Non-adaptive Item selection algorithms
    1. Fixed form
      Items on a fixed form segment or test are selected in the order prescribed in the itembank.
    2. Field test
      Field test items are selected on a distributional model, where the itemgroups administered least often have the highest priority, with random numbers used to break ties.
      Field test items are selected prior to the start of a test. They are removed from the pool in case a passage has a combination of field test and operational items.
